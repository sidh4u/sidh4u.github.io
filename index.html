👤 About Me

🛠️ These days, I’m knee-deep in Kubernetes, EKS, and automation—solving gnarly problems, building things that just work, and helping teams level up through better tools and mentoring. I’m currently shaping infrastructure at Caastle while keeping an eye out for the next opportunity to build something meaningful.

I’ve been part of both fast-moving startups and giants like Intuit, Nokia, Yahoo, and AOL—and I’m always game for the next bold challenge, especially if it involves building something meaningful from the ground up.

I also partner closely with leadership to shape tech strategy that aligns with business goals—usually with smart, open source choices that deliver serious ROI.

🧭 Outside of the terminal, I recharge by clicking shutters 📸, wandering off to new places ✈️, and putting brush to canvas 🎨. I’ve found that photography and sketching sharpen the same skills I use at work—observation, composition, and a little creative chaos.

Whether it’s tech or art, I’m all about bringing clarity to the messy and turning good ideas into great experiences. Let’s build something cool.
